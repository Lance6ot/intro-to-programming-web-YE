About Me

Welcome to my personal website. I am Ye Liu (柳烨), a student at Berklee College of Music studying Music Business. I specialize in the legal and commercial aspects of the music industry, but I am also passionate about Electronic Production & Design (EPD) and scoring for new media. My goal is to create unique musical experiences for film, games, and television.

This studio represents the starting point for my career goal: to found a scoring company that combines cutting-edge music technology with a deep understanding of composition (especially jazz and contemporary harmony) to deliver high-quality, bespoke music for clients.
